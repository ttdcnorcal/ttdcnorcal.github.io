THOMAS L. LOWE, MD

Distinguished Fellow, APA
MEDICAL DIRECTOR, TTDC

After 32 years on the full-time faculty of UCSF, Thomas L. Lowe. MD has retired from the University and formed the Center (TTDC) for the Bay Area. An internationally recognized expert on Tourette's & Tic Disorders, Dr. Lowe provides the highest quality consultation for patients of all ages, having worked with well over 1,000 TS patients and their families.

Dr. Lowe also served as a member of the Medical Advisory Board (MAB) of the National Tourette Syndrome Association (TSA) (1988-2000).

Dr. Lowe continues his long-term follow-up of Tourette's patients at UCSF. This on-going research provides a special perspective at the TTDC.
